privacy and terme

We take the privacy of our education club website very seriously and ensure that all personal information is kept confidential.

Your personal information, including your name, email address, and any other details you provide, will never be shared with third parties without your consent.

Our website uses industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or misuse.

Any data collected on our website is used only for the purpose of providing you with a better experience and improving our services.

We will not send you any unsolicited emails or promotional material unless you specifically opt-in to receive such communications.

Our website may use cookies to personalize your experience and remember your preferences, but this information is not shared with anyone else.

You have the right to request access to your personal information, as well as the right to request that it be updated or deleted if necessary.

We may collect anonymous usage data to analyze website traffic and improve our services, but this information cannot be used to identify individual users.

Our privacy policy is regularly updated to ensure that it complies with current regulations and best practices in data protection.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please don't hesitate to contact us for further information.

Our education club website is designed to be a safe and secure online platform for members to learn and connect with each other, and we prioritize the privacy and security of our members at all times.

We do not collect any sensitive information, such as financial or medical information, unless it is required for a specific purpose and with your explicit consent.

Any communication or interaction you have on our website with other members is private and will not be shared with anyone else without your permission.

We only use your personal information to provide you with the services you have requested, and we do not sell or trade your information to any third party.

We use encrypted connections to transmit data between our website and your device, ensuring that your information is protected from interception or theft.

Our website is compliant with relevant data protection laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for members residing in the European Union.

We take appropriate measures to protect the privacy of children under the age of 13, and do not knowingly collect personal information from them without parental consent.

We may use third-party services or plugins on our website, but we ensure that these services are also compliant with our privacy policy and maintain the same level of data protection and security as we do.

Our privacy policy is easily accessible on our website, and we encourage all members to read and understand it before using our services.

We are committed to being transparent and accountable about our data practices, and will promptly notify members of any changes or updates to our privacy policy.
